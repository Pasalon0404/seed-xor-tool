🔒 PSBT Signing Help

  1. Load PSBT: Upload your unsigned `.psbt` file or scan a QR code.
  2. Review: Check the transaction details carefully. Verify amounts and destination addresses.
  3. Enter Seed: Input your 12 or 24-word BIP39 seed phrase into the grid, scan a QR code, or use the Quick Import to load an `.skb` file.
  4. Passphrase: Optionally enter your BIP39 passphrase if you use one (auto-filled if imported).
  5. Sign: Click "Sign & Generate File" to locally sign the transaction.

⚠️ Security Notes

  • This tool signs transactions locally in your browser.
  • Verify all addresses and amounts match your expectations.
  • Clear all data using the "Clear All" button after signing.
