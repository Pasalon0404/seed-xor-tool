PSBT Signing Guide

1. Load PSBT — Upload the unsigned .psbt file from your coordinator wallet (Sparrow, Electrum, etc.) or scan the QR code. Animated UR-encoded QR codes are supported.

2. Review — Verify the destination addresses and amounts match what you expect. Change outputs are highlighted in teal.

3. Sign — Enter your seed phrase (or load from file / scan QR). Add your passphrase if you use one. Click "Add Signature" to sign.

4. Export — Download the signed PSBT or display it as a QR code to transfer to your coordinator or next signer.

This tool signs transactions locally in your browser. No data leaves this device. Clear all data after signing.
